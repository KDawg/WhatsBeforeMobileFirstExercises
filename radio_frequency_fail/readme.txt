Also open it on the mobile Safari browser on iOS.
The OS X-based simulator is fine.

You'll see a simple page filled with orange and 3 blue buttons
on top labeled "red", "blue", and "green".

Tap those buttons as you like.
On Chrome they have a simple yellow underline displaying
its selection state.

is drawn with a black background. It's also showing a black
border.
